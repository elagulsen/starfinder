How does it work?

The Star Finder takes into account your latitude and longitude, and matches it to Sky Quality Meter measurements taken around your area.
These measurements are used to determine how bright, or light polluted, the night sky is in your area.
Based on this information, as well as the position of the stars on the day you access this website, the Star Finder will generate a list of stars it thinks you might be able to see outside on a clear night!
